The SkyHost Platform

SkyHost offers a robust, scalable, and developer-friendly platform built to host your applications with zero downtime and lightning-fast deployment. Whether you're managing a personal project or an enterprise-grade app, SkyHost gives you the tools and flexibility to ship faster.

Scalable Hosting: Instantly scale your applications with our auto-scaling infrastructure—no manual intervention needed.

Git Integration: Deploy directly from your GitHub or GitLab repositories. Every push automatically builds and deploys your app.

Intuitive Dashboard: Manage deployments, logs, and resources with a clean, powerful interface that works on any device.

Managed Databases: Easily provision and scale MySQL, PostgreSQL, or MongoDB with secure backups and minimal configuration.

99.99% Uptime: Our global infrastructure ensures your app stays available—anytime, anywhere.

One-Click Deployment: Go live in seconds with our one-click deploy button, perfect for prototypes, MVPs, or full-scale products.
